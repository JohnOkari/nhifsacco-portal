ABOUT US

Since its incepption in and subsquent registration on 11th Janauary 2001 under the Cooperative Act CAP 490 of the Lawas of Kenya, NHIF Sacco society has experienced growth in its membership, deposits and loans. As at december 2012, the Sacco had a membership of 939, share capital of kshs 12,227, 500 and deposits of kshs 226,780,240. The loan balances as at December 2012 was kshs 283, 998, 608.

Development of strategic plan was necessary so as to enable NHIF Sacco Society Ltd deal with emerging challenges in the business environment for the next five years. Having re-examined its mandate and core functions, the Sacco has made its vision and mission statements together with its core values which will provide the required strategic direction. A detailed review of the analysis of strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats, and the stakeholders ’ expectations and expected inputs formed the basis for the identification of the strategic themes and objectives for the next five years and formulation of appropriate strategic.

The strategic themes identified are; competitive financial services for the members, optimal institutional capacity, and effective resources mobilization for sustained growth. The Sacco plans to tackle these themes through several objectives that are well defined in the plan. In addition, an implementation matrix was developed which covers the strategic objectives, expected out puts, activities, output indicators, implementing actors and time frame.

To ensure successful implementation of the strategic plan a monitoring, evaluation and reporting frame work which emphasizes on periodic performance reviews was put in place. In addition, the Sacco commits to implement the plan by ensuring annual allocation of funds to support the same.
